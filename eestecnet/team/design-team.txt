EESTEC Design Team is responsible for EESTEC’s promotional materials’ creation based

on the rules in our Branding Guide. The general design needs by the bodies, organizing

committees and teams of EESTEC are replied by Design Team members who have knowledge

about graphic design and interested in EESTEC’s improvement as a brand, in other words, the

visual image of EESTEC is strongly supported by Design Team. While contributing to EESTEC

Design Team with their talents and innovative ideas, team members also find the opportunity to

improve themselves by learning a lot from other members of the team.
